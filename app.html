Why we built this
01
Young people live alone now
More Australians under 35 live alone than ever before. Share houses dissolve. Cities are big. No one automatically notices when you go quiet.
02
Older adults deserve independence
Your parents or grandparents shouldn't have to move in with someone just to feel safe. Still Here lets them stay independent without anyone worrying.
03
Existing apps are cold and clinical
Medical alert buttons. GPS trackers. Surveillance dressed up as care. We wanted something that felt human — a quiet daily ritual, not a device strapped to your wrist.
04
One tap. That's all it takes.
We built Still Here because we believe the simplest things matter most. Check in. Let someone know. Go about your day. It's enough.
